I approach every charm born from our encounters with sincerity,
embracing its essence and embodying the entire world it reflects.

Each step becomes a stage,
and the rhythm of breath and gaze leaves a gentle, beautiful echo in the space.

With the heat and heartbeat of that moment,
I carefully craft expressions that remain in memory, one by one, with devotion.

I am deeply grateful for this connection.
